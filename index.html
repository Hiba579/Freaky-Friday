Anemone:

Anemone includes 120 species of flowers,but the most common types found in North America come in white,pink or violet hues.The Anemone is dainty and doesn't grow well during summer droughts,overly wet winters or high winds.

Coleus :

This shade tolerant annual is known for its boldly patterned leaves and makes for a great border along gardens. Depending on the specie, the Coleus' leaves can come in shades of red.

Susan:

as the official flower for the Preakness, Blackeyed Susans are a member of the sunflower family. In addition to making a beautiful indoor flower arrangement, Blackeyed Susans attract butterflies.
